1. Overview

NullDev ("Company", "we", "us") respects the privacy of users of Three Habits ("App", "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect the following information:

  • Advertising Identifier: Advertising ID is collected for ad delivery through Google AdMob
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language settings
  • App Usage Information: App launch count, usage time, error logs

2.2 User-Provided Information

We collect the following information directly from users:

  • Habit Data: Habit names and check records entered by users

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect the following information:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email, phone number
  • Location information
  • Device data such as contacts, photos, files

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Providing habit tracking functionality
  • Ad Display: Delivering personalized ads through Google AdMob
  • App Improvement: Bug fixes and performance improvements
  • Usage Analysis: Analyzing app usage patterns to improve the service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Habit data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. All data is completely deleted when you uninstall the app.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Ad delivery (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. However, exceptions apply in the following cases:

  • Compliance with legal requirements
  • Third-party services for service provision (Google AdMob)

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:
